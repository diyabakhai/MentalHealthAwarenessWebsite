Quick Tips


Stress

  1. Exercise
  2. Deep Breathing
  3. Take a Break
  4. Talk About Your Problems
  5. Try and eliminate Your Triggers

Anxiety

  1. Question your thought pattern
  2. Practice focused, deep breathing
  3. Use Aromatherapy
  4. Write down your thoughts
  5. Do a daily or routine meditation

Depression

  1. Talk to one person about your feelings.
  2. Meet new people by taking a class or joining a club.
  3. Confide in a loved one.
  4. Accompany someone to the movies or a small get-together.
  5. If it's getting excessive,consult a psychologist.

Overthinking


  1. Change The Story You Tell Yourself
  2. Let Go of The Past
  3. Stop Your Thoughts in The Moment and Practice Being Present
  4. Focus on What You Can Control
  5. Write Down (or Openly Share) Solutions (Not Problems)

Mood Swings

  1. Get sufficient sleep
  2. Eat a healthy diet.
  3. Practice relaxation
  4. Express yourself, Take it out.
  5. Avoid stress.

Languishing

  1. Focus on physical well-being.
  2. Try Journaling
  3. Exploring creativity
  4. Learning new skills
  5. Seeking professional help
